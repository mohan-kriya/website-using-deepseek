Neem Oil Technology

Margoshine products are derived from premium quality neem seeds through cold-pressing technology that preserves the natural bioactive compounds. Neem oil contains multiple active ingredients including azadirachtin, nimbin, and salanin that work synergistically to provide repellent, anti-feedant, growth regulatory, and oviposition deterrent effects on various pests.
