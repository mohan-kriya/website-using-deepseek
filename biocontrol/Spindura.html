Spinosad Technology

Spinosad is derived from the fermentation of Saccharopolyspora spinosa, a naturally occurring soil bacterium. It acts on the insect's nervous system, causing excitation of the nervous system, leading to involuntary muscle contractions, tremors, and paralysis. This unique mode of action makes it an excellent tool for resistance management.
